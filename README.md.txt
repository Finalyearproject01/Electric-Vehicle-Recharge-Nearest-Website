About This Project
Transportation electrification is one of the essential components in the future of smart city planning and electric vehicles will be integrated into the transportation system seamlessly. In this paper, we formulate the Electric Vehicle Charging Slot Booking, in which we minimize the charging station queue for EV charging. The proposed system of the EV Charging mobile website provides EV car owners the convenience of locating charging stations on Google Maps, vacancy of charging slots, and getting status updates on charging.
